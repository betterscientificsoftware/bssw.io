Join us for the next webinar for the National Laboratories Sustainable Scientific Software Conference's new Community of Practice, as Rob Kahler gives us a brief, lighthearted introduction to the concept of Test-Driven Development, including a short example and a discussion of why this specific concept and rigorous testing in general are vital for effective software engineering.
